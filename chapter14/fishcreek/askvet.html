Question: Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to each chocolate?
Answer: Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog chocolate. Try playing a game with your children — when you feed them people treates, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
